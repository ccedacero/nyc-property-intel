# NYC RE attorney LinkedIn DM template

**Audience:** NYC real estate attorneys, due-diligence consultants, transactional lawyers.
**Goal:** Personal outreach with demo. Attorneys are highest-LTV users.
**Strategy:** 20 personalized DMs. NOT an email blast. Each one references their actual practice.

## How to find prospects

LinkedIn search:
- "Real estate attorney" + "New York"
- "Due diligence" + "NYC"
- "Real estate counsel" + "NYC"

Filter by: Greater New York Area, 2nd-degree connections (they're more likely to respond), Active in last 30 days.

Aim for 20 well-targeted DMs over a single afternoon.

## Personalization examples (good vs bad)

**Bad (templated):**
> Hi Sarah, I'm reaching out because you're a real estate attorney in NYC.

**Good (specific):**
> Hi Sarah — I saw your CLE talk on AML compliance for high-value transactions. The ACRIS lien check you described is exactly what NYC Property Intel automates.

**Best (mutual context):**
> Hi Sarah — we connected last fall through [name]. I remember you mentioning the time-sink of pre-acquisition due diligence on multifamily portfolios. Built something for that.

## Follow-up cadence

- DM 1: pitch (above)
- Day +5: if no reply, soft bump — "Hey, no pressure, just bumping in case this got buried"
- Day +12: if no reply, drop it. Don't be a stalker.
- If they reply with interest: schedule the Zoom within 5 business days

## What to ask in the Zoom (20 min)

- What's their actual due-diligence workflow today?
- What datasets are most important to them? Least?
- What output format do they want (Markdown report, PDF, integrated into their case management software)?
- What would they pay for? (Per-property? Subscription? Per-user?)
- Would they be a beta partner if we offered them free access for 6 months?

## What NOT to do

- Don't spam. 20 DMs total, not 200.
- Don't pitch + immediately ask to schedule a call. Lead with value (demo URL).
- Don't claim "AI replaces your due-diligence." It augments it. Attorneys are protective of their billable hours; frame as "make your associates faster," not "replace your associates."
- Don't BCC anyone. Don't use a CRM that drips automated follow-ups. They smell that.
- Don't pitch tenant screening / Fair Housing-adjacent uses. Lead with transactional / acquisition work.

## Goal metrics

- 20 DMs sent
- 4–6 replies (20–30% reply rate is good for cold)
- 2–3 Zoom calls
- 1 bona-fide power user / pilot customer

If you get to 1 paying attorney + 1 referral chain by end of month, that's a win.
